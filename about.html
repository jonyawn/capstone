The Mission

With this project I am hoping to bring more awareness and understanding to mental health issues. This is also intended to give people resources to help with their mental illnesses. Specifically this website is focused mostly on local mental health resources in the Greensboro area.

Want to learn more?

If you would like to find national, local, and university resources for mental health then click on the Resources page, there you can find services to help you with your mental health. Included is a brief description of each resource and what they offer.
