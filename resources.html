National Resources

    • Suicide Hotline- If you are in a crisis and need to talk someone dial or text 988 for the suicide hotline. You will be connected with a counselor who you can talk to about whatever mental health problem you are struggling with whether it be substance abuse, depression, anxiety, grief, and many other things that one might struggle with.
    • National Alliance on Mental Illness- The National Alliance on Mental Illness offers support and resources to those struggling with mental health problems. These resources include offering counseling and recommendations to services across the United States and can be called or texted to get access to their counseling services.
    • Gambling Addiction Hotline- The National Council on Problem Gambling is a useful resource for anyone who is addicted to gambling. The main resource offered is via phone at the number 1-800-522-4700. Calling the service one can get access to a counselor who can help talk with anyone who is addicted.
    • Domestic Violence Hotline- If you are struggling with a domestic violence situation then the domestic violence hotline is a useful tool for finding local shelters and counseling resources. The website linked will give access to online resources, and for phone support dial 800-799-7233 or text BEGIN to 88788.

Local Resources

    • Guilford County Behavioral Health Center- The Guilford County Behavior Center is open 24/7 and is for those going through a mental health or substance abuse crisis. Anyone can go to the center for assistance and no appointment is needed. People who are unsure as to whether they should go can call ahead at the phone number listed on the page.
    • Cone Health Behavioral Hospital- The Cone Health Behavioral hospital is available for those who are recommended to the facility by counselor or psychiatrist. The facility does not offer walk in services.
    • Alcohol and Drug Services- The Alcohol and Drug Services gives resources for those struggling with addiction. There are a host of different locations that people can go to for rehabilitation.

University Resources

  • UNCG

    • Counseling and Psychological Services- UNCG offers a wide variety of services for students and employees regarding psychological services. These services include access to counseling and psychologists as well as support services and crisis counseling.
    • Togetherall- Togetherall is an online mental health resource in which participants can anonymously share their feelings and access mental health resources online. It is good if you feel uncomfortable sharing in person or on the phone and is available to all university students and staff for use.
    • Spartan Recovery Program- The Spartan Recovery program is a good resource for university students who are struggling with addiction and offers a variety of resources for those in recovery. Currently these resources include: recovery housing, recovery coaching, and referral to other recovery resources if needed.

  • A&T

    • Counseling Services- A&T offers many resources through its counseling services program these include: counseling, a recovery program that strives to support students struggling with their mental health by offering resources for counseling as well as other more specific resources that require more intense care or treatment.
    • Togetherall- Togetherall is an online mental health resource in which participants can anonymously share their feelings and access mental health resources online. It is good if you feel uncomfortable sharing in person or on the phone and is available to all university students and staff for use.
    • Collegiate Recovery Community- A&T offers recovery resources to students who may be dealing with addiction and trying to get treatment for it. Some of the resources offered throught the program include safe spaces to meet other students currently in the recovery process as well as events and meetings for students who are going through treatment.

  • Greensboro College

    • Counseling Services- Greensboro college offers counseling services to all students and staff and appointments can be made via email or phone call if needed. The counseling services assist with things like anxiety, depression, and substance abuse. The page linked includes a long list of national and local resources, however the university only offers counseling services to students.

  • Bennett College

    • Counseling Services- Bennett college offers counseling services to all students and staff, appointments can be made for counseling online or via phone. The services cover things such as deoression, anxiety, sexuality, and substance abuse issues. The intake form for the counseling services is linked on the counseling page and can be used at any time.
    • TimelyCare- TimelyCare is an online service offered by Bennett college that includes basic medical services as well as psychiatric and counseling if needed. Appointments can be made online and the service is offered to all students and staff of the university.

  • Guilford College

    • Milner Student Health, Welness, and Counseling- Guilford college offers mental health consultations as well as short term counseling and crisis intervention. Students and staff can access these resources through email or via phone call. The service also offers a psychiatrist if there is need for medication or other treatment that normal counselors cannot provide.
